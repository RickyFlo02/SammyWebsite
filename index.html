Who Are We?

Sigma Alpha Mu - Delta Omega chapter at RIT is dedicated to instilling strong fraternal values, offering social and service opportunities, encouraging academic excellence and teaching leadership skills through brotherhood.

Known as “Sammy” on campus, we run philanthropy events like PoleSit and Run for the Fund to raise money for the Judy Fund and other local charities. Our chapter focuses on building and fostering men from all walks of life. We pride ourselves on helping young men grow and follow our values of True Manhood, Democracy, and Humanity.
